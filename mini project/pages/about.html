ABOUT ACAI






ACAIThe Association of Computer Applications and Intellectuals is an Students Oraganization which is established in 2022.
ACAI was initiated by the Students of Bachelor Of Computer Applications(B.C.A)to enhance the ability of students and their
talents in a way by conducting Activities and Events.The ACAI Association was opened by the PrincipalDr.V.Radhakrishnan (KSRCAS) and The Head of Department (BCA)Dr.T.S.VENKATESWARAN M.Sc,M.Phil.,MBA,M.Phil.,Ph.D
